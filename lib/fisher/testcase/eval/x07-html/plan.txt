This produces a concordance of sorts.  
Input: a corpus of HTML text.
Output: 
  (a) A version of the corpus with <a name="XXX"/> tags added all over the place.
  (b) A concordance: a HTML table listing all the words in the corpus, 
      in alphabetical order, plus columns of 
      - number of times it appears in the corpus.
      - Hyperlinks to all the occurrences.
